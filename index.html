Event Guideline



Before moving onto the registration form, take a look at the problem statements, and note down the problem-code.

REGISTRATION:

  • The participant must have completed atleast one course/workshop with Lema Labs ( KRP , ML/IOT workshop).
  • Every team must register only once
  • One representative from each team must register the team with with the team name and list of members.
  • A team can have a minimum of 3 members, upto a maximum of 5 members.
  • Individuals registering without a team will be assigned to a team with similar interests.
  • Teams with different team-names but same team-members will be disqualified
  • While filling the form please read through the descriptions and then fill the form
  • Last date for registration 16/12/2019

PROBLEM STATEMENT:

  • Teams can come up their own problem statements from the given domains else can choose from the problem statements given by us during registration.
  • The selection of teams for a particular problem statements will be on First Come First Serve basis.
  • Once a particular problem is selected by a team, it becomes unavailable for the rest.
  • All participants will be given a participation certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions ?

What is Makeathon ?
A Makeathon has it’s focus on the process of making things together. It is more about the progress than it is about the end result. The main drivers for a Makeathon are 4 folds :
  1. Having fun creating stuff: Creating something new is an exhilarating experience.
  2. Learning while you go: The best way to learn is to build something.
  3. Increase your network reach: Professional and Non-Professional friendships are easily formed in the right environment.
  4. Aaaaand Most of All: Lip smacking food!! XD
Who can participate ?
Anybody who is a part of Lema Community is eligible to take part in the Make-a-thon (Anyone who has attended IOT workshop/Kaizen robotics program/Machine Learning courses conducted by Lema Labs qualify as a member of the Lema Community :)
How many team members can I have?
A team can have a minimum of 3 members to a maximum of 5 members.
Can I start working on my hack before the event ?
Nope! Even the most primitive of connections are to be made only after the commencement of the event. However, you can install softwares and packages, make prototype outlines and model before the event. You can even discuss with your respective mentors and plan on how to go about it!
I don't have a teammate, can I register?
We have implemented a very effective auto matching system (no, NOT TINDER!), so just in case you are in need of a team, worry not, we've got your back.
